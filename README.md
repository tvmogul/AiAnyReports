Just give **AiAnyReports®** your database connection string and ask in plain English:
*“Write me an operations report and point out inefficiencies, bottlenecks, and unusual results.”*
**Done.**

**AiAnyReports®** transforms raw databases into both standard business reports and a new class of **AI Reports** that identify what matters, what is changing, what looks unusual, and where leaders should focus next. By analyzing schema structure, field names, and relationships, it automatically writes the SQL needed to generate finished, polished, professional reports—making it one of the biggest breakthroughs in report creation since the spreadsheet.

Running 100% locally on your machine, my AI reads your entire database schema, understands the business context, and generates production-ready SQL with joins, aggregations, filters, comments, and anomaly detection—then turns that SQL into beautiful, presentation-ready reports in seconds.

Powered by my new **Zero-Training AI™**, this app runs 100% offline on Mac and Windows, requires **NO data and NO training**, and **never sends your data to the cloud**. It analyzes your own data locally, generates advanced AI-driven insights that standard AI can't, and turns them into finished reports that reveal what **NO programmer could code by hand**: hidden risks, overlooked opportunities, and where more money can be made.

Your team or executives can now create any report in seconds instead of waiting weeks for custom development.
**Operations →** “Show me Q1 throughput by machine with variance flags”
**Sales →** “Compare regional revenue YoY for active customers only”
**Finance →** “Break down expenses by vendor with tax-deductible highlights”

**AiAnyReports®** creates just two files—a DLL and a compact data file—that you drop into your server or mobile app. Instantly, you get every significant report on your data, plus any report you request in plain English. Reports are rendered with the latest jQuery DataTables and support rich customization and exports including Print, PDF, and Excel.

I built this because I was tired of watching companies spend huge amounts of money and months of delay on custom reports that become outdated the moment the business changes.
